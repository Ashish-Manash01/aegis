### "Connecting Global Sourcing Hedging directly to the Smart Shopfloor"

* **The Sourcing Brief Summary**: 
  * Volatile geopolitics, canal delays, and trade tariffs halt production lines. Concurrently, factory floors lose millions due to manual inspection lag and delayed welding defect detection.
* **The Proposed Solution (AegisFlow AI)**:

  * **Core A: AltRoute-AI (Sourcing)**: Moniters ports/straits, maps raw battery materials, and calculates alternate routing sea-lanes and domestic sourcing options (Atacama Chile & Pune hubs).

AegisFlow AI *connects them in a closed-loop system*—automatically issuing ERP reorder requests and robotic guide-rail calibrations upon defect detection.
